# 🧠 AI Learning Prompt Template

## 🎯 Purpose
To generate a comprehensive and well-structured learning roadmap on a specific topic, with both theoretical depth and practical application, in order to build real-world projects or solutions.

---

## 🧩 Prompt Template

You are an expert AI tutor and technical writer.

I want to deeply understand the topic of **[INSERT TOPIC]**.

Please generate a comprehensive **Learning Roadmap** for this topic in the form of a well-structured `.md` (Markdown) file.

### The output must include:

#### 📚 Theoretical Foundations:
- Clear definitions of key terms and concepts
- Step-by-step progression from beginner to advanced levels
- Explanation of core principles, mechanisms, and subtopics
- Best practices and common pitfalls

#### 🧰 Tools & Resources:
- Recommended courses, books, articles, and videos
- Essential tools, libraries, and frameworks related to the topic

#### 🧪 Practical Application:
- Code examples or pseudocode
- Project ideas to reinforce learning
- Use cases and real-world examples
- Insights into current trends and future developments

Make sure the roadmap is detailed, actionable, and easy to follow for a self-directed learner.
